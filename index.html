About College

"In the academic year 2016-17, considering the increasing number of post-10th students in Ratnagiri district, Ratnagiri Education Society started R. B. Shirke Junior College on 14th July 2016. The college was inaugurated by the then-working President Adv. Patne, along with Adv. Vijay Sakhalkar, Mrs. Prachi Joshi (Actress), and Governing Body Member Shri Manoj Patankar."
